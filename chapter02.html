Chapter 2

As Boss takes the keys from Tommy, the unexpected guests arrive - the cops. Everyone scatters into all possible directions apart Tommy. Having lost his car, he has no way of escaping and is arrested by Sergeant 'Cross' whose aim is to end the street racing scene. However, in the unlikeliest of situations, Tommy is released because Sergeant does not have sufficient evidence about him. Kelly, who was one of spectators during the race picks him up from the police department and helps him by providing a garage to work on his cars. As they arrive into the hideout, she explains that the only way to call out a racer from the Outlawz list is to beat the targets set by the racer who is above you in the list. Having finished explaining everything, she gives Tommy a lump of money to get himself a new car. Without wasting any more time, Tommy heads out to one of the car dealers in California and buys himself a stock Pontiac GTO. After modifying the car with the remaining budget, he receives a message from Kelly with the initial tasks. Participating in illegal races, destroying the town and making a bad name with the police. This is where the real journey for Tommy begins.
