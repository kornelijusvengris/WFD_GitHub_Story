Chapter 3

As he cracks through the objectives, Tommy soon finds himself as one of the main targets for the cops due to his bad name. The races become a lot more difficult as the number of cops patrolling around the state of Cali increases. Having completed his first set of objectives, he calls out the #15 on the list. The meeting place and time is agreed and without wasting any time he makes his way to the location. Having arrived, Tommy senses that something does not feel right. He cautiously waits at the location for a couple of minutes expecting the #15 to arrive, but the unexpected guests burst in once again - Cross among several other police men surround him. It becomes evident to Tommy that someone had led him into a trap. Cross gleefully arrests Tommy and brings him to the police station. This time Cross is ecstatic as he finally has enough proof to keep him locked up. While Tommy sits in his cell, he hears his name being called out. There is someone on the phone for him. Once the phone is picked up, an evil laugh can be heard. Tommy realises that it was the same racer who took his car, has now set him up. Boss explains that some no-name like Tommy will not be tolerated on his streets and hangs up. Cross eventually agrees to cut Tommy's sentence short but only on one condition - he is to be banned from America and must return to Europe.
