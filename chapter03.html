Chapter 3

As he cracks down on the objectives, Tommy soon finds himself as one of the main targets for the cops. The races become a lot more difficult due to an increase of cops patrolling around the state of Cali. Once Tommy completes his first set of objectives, he calls out the #15 on the list. The meeting place and time is agreed an he makes his way to the location. Having arrived, Tommy senses that something does not feel right. He waits at the location for a couple of minutes expecting to see the #15, but the unexpected arrives - Cross among several other police men. Tommy was led into a trap by someone. Cross gleefully arrests Tommy and brings him to the police station. Cross is ecstatic as he finally has enough proof to keep him locked up this time. As Tommy sits in his cell, he is called up by one of the policemen. There is someone on the phone for him. Once the phone is picked up, there is an evil laugh. Tommy realises that it was the racer Boss who set him up. Boss explains that some no-name like Tommy will not be tolerated in the scene and hangs up. Cross eventually agrees to cut Tommy's sentence short but with one condition, he is to be banned from America and return to Europe.
